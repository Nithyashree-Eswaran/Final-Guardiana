Why is it necessary to detect disease in plant ?

Detecting plant diseases is crucial for their growth, productivity, and overall well-being. If untreated, these issues can spread quickly, damaging plants, soil, and the ecosystem. Like silent friends, plants need attentive care. By monitoring their health and detecting problems early, we can provide the necessary treatment, helping them recover and thrive. As plant caretakers, it's our responsibility to observe changes in color, shape, or behavior, preventing disease spread and promoting healthy growth. Let's be the plant doctors they need, ensuring a bountiful harvest with love, care, and attention.
